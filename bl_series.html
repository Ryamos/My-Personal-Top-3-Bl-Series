My Top 3 Bl Series

    Boy love (BL) series offer representation for LGBTQ+ individuals, providing visibility to relationships that are often underrepresented in mainstream media. Seeing diverse relationships on screen can be validating and empowering for LGBTQ+ viewers

    1. Bad Buddy

    Based on the novel Behind The Scenes by Afterday and -West-, it tells the story of two boys who came from warring, neighboring families and found themselves competing for almost everything, but developed romantic feelings for each other along the way.


    2. Together The System

    A student named Tine wants to get rid of an unwanted admirer so his friends recommend getting a pretend boyfriend. Though reluctant at first, Sarawat finally agrees and the two become close despite their relationship not being real.


    3. We Are

    For this friend group, no matter how hard their university studies are, there's always something more to do their head in; the chaos of life and love won't let anyone rest. We are... friends, but we fight each other and like each other so much we might hit on each other.
